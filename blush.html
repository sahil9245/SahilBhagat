Overview

Blush is a new online tool created by Pablo Stanley that holds a collection of high​-quality illustrations from selected artists around the world.

I get a chance to work with the Blush team and created my series of cycling, running, workout and Yoga. Blush is managed by a complex system that offers a high level of Illustrations. For Each theme, I have created 80+ layers to offer infinite illustration combinations.
