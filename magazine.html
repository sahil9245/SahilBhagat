Overview

Indian Private Equity and Venture Capital Association (Promoting Private Capital Ecosystem) launched their first annual magazine. It was a mega launch with the presence of media across the country.

I was privileged to work on the designs. It was a month-long project with the stakeholders from IVCA and Dharma media.  

There are many essential components to take care of while working on this project:
- Digital Look to Print Layouts
- Readability
- Hierarchy
- Balance
- Typography
